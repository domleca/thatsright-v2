Step 3 of 6

Tell the assistant about your parent

  1. The assistant's questions appear on screen, one at a time: your parent's life, the people who matter to them, their daily habits, their health. Answer by speaking, as you would in a conversation. You can also type any answer instead of saying it.
  2. At the first question, iOS asks for access to the microphone. Tap Allow so the assistant can hear you.
  3. Take your time. The conversation lasts about 5 to 10 minutes, but you can close the app at any point and come back. It remembers exactly where you were.
  4. "I don't know" is always a fine answer. The assistant notes it and moves on. You can fill in the detail later from the family group.
You should see One question at a time on a calm screen, a circle that reacts while you speak, and a progress indicator at the top.
