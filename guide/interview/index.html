Step 3 of 7

Tell the assistant about your parent

  1. The app chats with you in a calm, iMessage-style conversation, asking gentle questions about your parent: their life, the people who matter to them, their daily habits, their health.
  2. Take your time. The conversation lasts about 15 to 20 minutes, but you can close the app at any point and come back. It remembers exactly where you were.
  3. "I don't know" is always a fine answer. The assistant notes it and moves on. You can fill in the detail later from the family group.
You should see A conversation screen with the assistant's questions appearing one by one, and a text field for your answers.
